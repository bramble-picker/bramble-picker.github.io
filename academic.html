Research Interests

I'm a PhD researcher interested in comics. The research questions I'm interested in are: Why do comic writers and artists decide to structure their work the way that they do? What sort of decisions do they make in their process of comics creation?

At the moment, I'm using computational methods to investigate this. I am annotating a comics corpus to demarcate how all space on each comic page is used. Then I'll try to describe how the areas relate to each other using constraint solving methods.

Overall, I would like to better understand how thoughts can manifest in visual sequences, or notations. I have a background in philosophy, sociology, and art practice. I've completed an MA in Language and Cognition at King's College London and an MA in Sociology at Bielefeld University in Germany.
